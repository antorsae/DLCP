HFD v4.96 and firmware v1.42 release notes:
- Firmware improvements (v1.42):
	- NCAS500 and NCAS1000 implemented as amplifiers
	- Equalizer filters now have the same precision as the channel filters (double precision)
	
- HFD improvements (v4.96):
	- NCAS500 and NCAS1000 soft clip limiter updated
	- NCAS500 can not have third channel enabled
